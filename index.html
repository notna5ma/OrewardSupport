About Oreward

Oreward is a quick endless digging game where every dig counts. Collect ore, push deeper, and try to beat your best score before your digs run out.

Common Help

The game is not loading

Close and reopen the app. If it still does not work, restart your device and try again.

I found a bug

Email support with your device model, iOS version, and what happened.

My score did not save

Make sure you are using the latest version of the app. Then contact support if it keeps happening.
